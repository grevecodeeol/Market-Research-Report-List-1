<p><ul><li>Active Omnidirectional Antenna</li><li>Active Directional Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The active antennas market comprises two main types: active omnidirectional antennas and active directional antennas. Active omnidirectional antennas emit signals uniformly in all directions, making them ideal for applications requiring broad coverage, such as urban wireless networks. In contrast, active directional antennas focus signals in a specific direction, enhancing signal strength and range for targeted communications, often used in point-to-point links or long-distance transmissions. Both types incorporate amplifiers for improved performance, catering to diverse connectivity needs across various industries.</p></p>
