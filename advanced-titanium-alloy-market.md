<p><p>The advanced titanium alloy market is segmented into three main types: α Titanium Alloys, β Titanium Alloys, and α+β Titanium Alloys. α Titanium Alloys, known for their excellent corrosion resistance and high-temperature strength, are widely used in aerospace applications. β Titanium Alloys offer enhanced ductility and strength, making them ideal for applications requiring high impact resistance. α+β Titanium Alloys combine properties of both types, providing versatility for various industries, including medical, automotive, and military, where performance and reliability are crucial.</p></p>
