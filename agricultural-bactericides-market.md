<p><p>The agricultural bactericides market is categorized into several types based on their chemical composition and function. Copper-based bactericides, known for their broad-spectrum efficacy, are often used to control bacterial diseases in crops. Dithiocarbamates serve as multi-functional fungicides and bactericides, offering preventive action. Amide-based bactericides work by inhibiting bacterial cell division and metabolism. Together, these products cater to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
