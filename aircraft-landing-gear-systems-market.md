<p><ul><li>Strut Landing Gear</li><li>Rocker Landing Gear</li><li>Pontoon Landing Gear</li><li>Framed Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft landing gear systems market includes various types, each designed for specific operational needs. Strut landing gear features long, straight supports for stability on various terrains. Rocker landing gear utilizes a pivot mechanism, providing a smoother ride over uneven surfaces. Pontoon landing gear is tailored for amphibious aircraft, allowing land and water operation. Framed landing gear consists of a supportive framework, often used in heavier aircraft to distribute weight and enhance durability. Each type plays a crucial role in aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
