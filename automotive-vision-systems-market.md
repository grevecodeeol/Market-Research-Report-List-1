Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) automatically adjusts speed to maintain safe distances from other vehicles. Blind Spot Detection (BSD) alerts drivers to vehicles in their blind spots, and Lane Departure Warning (LDW) warns when drifting out of lanes. Together, these systems contribute to safer driving experiences.</p></p>
