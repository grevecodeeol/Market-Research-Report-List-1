<p><ul><li>Oxidoreductases</li><li>Transferases</li><li>Hydrolases</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biocatalysts market is categorized into four primary types: oxidoreductases, transferases, hydrolases, and others. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, crucial in metabolic processes. Transferases transfer functional groups between molecules, playing a vital role in various biosynthetic pathways. Hydrolases catalyze the breakdown of compounds through hydrolysis, significant in digestion and biotransformation. The "others" category encompasses less common classes of enzymes, including ligases and isomerases, which are essential for specific biochemical processes, broadening the applications in industrial and pharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
