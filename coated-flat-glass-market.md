<p><p>The coated flat glass market comprises several key types, including ordinary flat glass, float glass, and rolled glass. Ordinary flat glass is typically used in windows and buildings due to its transparent properties. Float glass is produced by floating molten glass on tin, resulting in a smooth, clear surface ideal for high-quality applications. Rolled glass, created by passing molten glass through rollers, offers a textured surface suitable for decorative purposes and enhanced privacy. Each type serves distinct applications in construction, automotive, and design industries.</p></p>
