<p><p>Color Doppler ultrasound systems are specialized imaging tools that assess blood flow and cardiac function. They are categorized into handheld and trolley-based systems. Handheld Doppler ultrasound systems are portable, offering convenience for point-of-care assessments, particularly in emergency and outpatient settings. In contrast, trolley-based Doppler ultrasound systems are larger, equipped with advanced features and imaging capabilities, typically used in hospitals and clinics for comprehensive diagnostic evaluations. Both types enhance non-invasive cardiovascular assessment and patient monitoring.</p></p>
