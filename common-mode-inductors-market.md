<p><strong>The Common Mode Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Common Mode Choke Coils</li><li>Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The common mode inductors market is primarily divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) common mode choke coils and through-hole common mode choke coils. SMD choke coils are designed for compact electronic applications, allowing for efficient mounting on circuit boards, minimizing space usage. In contrast, through-hole choke coils are suited for applications requiring higher power handling and stability, typically featuring leads that penetrate the PCB. Both types are essential for filtering noise and improving signal integrity in various electronic devices.</p></p>
