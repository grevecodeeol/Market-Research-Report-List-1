<p><ul><li>ETP (Electrolytic Tough Pitch Copper Busbar)</li><li>OF (Oxygen-free Copper Busbar)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper busbar market is segmented into three primary types: ETP, OF, and others. ETP (Electrolytic Tough Pitch) copper busbars are known for high conductivity and excellent mechanical properties, making them suitable for electrical power distribution. OF (Oxygen-free) copper busbars are characterized by minimal oxygen content, providing superior performance in critical applications where heat and corrosion resistance are essential. The "Others" category encompasses various specialized alloys and treatments, catering to niche applications in industries such as telecommunications and renewable energy.</p></p>
