<p><ul><li>Magnetorheological Damper</li><li>Hydraulic Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The damper test system market includes two prominent types: magnetorheological dampers and hydraulic dampers. Magnetorheological dampers utilize fluids that change viscosity in response to magnetic fields, allowing for real-time control over damping characteristics, making them ideal for advanced applications. In contrast, hydraulic dampers rely on the flow of fluid through valves to absorb energy, offering reliability and simplicity for various automotive and industrial applications. Both types are crucial for enhancing performance, safety, and comfort in vehicles and machinery.</p></p>
