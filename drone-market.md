Spiral Wing drones, often used for specialized tasks, have a unique design that enhances maneuverability and stability in various environments. Helicopter drones, or multi-rotors, provide excellent vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them ideal for aerial photography and applications requiring agility in confined spaces. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
