<p><p>The elemental fertilizer market comprises three main types: nitrogen fertilizers, phosphate fertilizers, and potash fertilizers. Nitrogen fertilizers, derived mainly from ammonia, enhance plant growth and protein synthesis. Phosphate fertilizers, usually derived from phosphate rock, promote root development and flowering. Potash fertilizers, sourced from potassium salts, improve drought resistance and overall plant health. Together, these fertilizers are essential for optimizing crop yields and sustaining agricultural productivity, addressing the nutritional needs of various plants across different soil types and climates.</p></p>
