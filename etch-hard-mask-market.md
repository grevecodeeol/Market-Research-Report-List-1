<p><p>The Etch Hard Mask market comprises two primary types: metal oxide-based and carbon-based masks. Metal oxide-based masks, such as titanium oxide and aluminum oxide, offer excellent thermal stability and resistance to etching, making them suitable for high-precision applications. Carbon-based masks, including amorphous carbon and diamond-like carbon, provide high hardness and chemical resistance, ideal for protectively coating surfaces during etching processes. Both types play critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, enabling intricate patterning and enhancing device performance.</p></p>
