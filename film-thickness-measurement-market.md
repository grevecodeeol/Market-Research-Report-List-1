<p><strong>The Film Thickness Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thickness Monitor</li><li>Spectrum Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The film thickness measurement market includes various technologies, notably thickness monitors and spectrum ellipsometers. Thickness monitors utilize techniques like interference and quartz crystal microbalance to gauge layer thickness in real-time during deposition processes. In contrast, spectrum ellipsometers measure the change in polarization of light reflected from a film surface to determine thickness and optical properties with high precision. Both methods are essential in industries such as semiconductors and coatings, ensuring optimal material performance and quality in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
