<p><strong>The Frozen Skin Graft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split-thickness</li><li>Full-thickness</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen skin graft market is categorized into two primary types: split-thickness and full-thickness grafts. Split-thickness grafts involve the removal of the epidermis and a portion of the dermis, making them suitable for larger areas, promoting quicker healing, and reducing the chance of complications. In contrast, full-thickness grafts contain the entire dermis and epidermis, offering improved aesthetics and ideal for smaller, more precise applications. Each type serves specific clinical needs, influencing choice based on the wound's characteristics and location.</p></p>
