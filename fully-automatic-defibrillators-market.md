<p><p>The Fully Automatic Defibrillators market encompasses various types, primarily including Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) and Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs). AEDs are portable devices designed for use by laypersons in emergencies, providing automated guidance for defibrillation. ICDs, on the other hand, are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor the heart and deliver shocks when arrhythmias are detected. Other devices in this market may include wearable defibrillators and advanced cardiac monitors, contributing to diverse solutions for cardiac emergencies and management.</p></p>
