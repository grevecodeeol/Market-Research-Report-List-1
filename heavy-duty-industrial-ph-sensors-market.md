<p><p>The Heavy Duty Industrial pH Sensors market primarily includes Glass Sensors and ISFET Sensors. Glass Sensors are traditional devices that use a glass electrode to measure pH levels, known for their accuracy and reliability in various environments. ISFET Sensors, on the other hand, utilize Ion-Sensitive Field Effect Transistor technology, providing advantages like faster response times and robustness against harsh conditions. Both sensor types are crucial for industries requiring precise pH monitoring, such as water treatment, chemical processing, and food production.</p></p>
