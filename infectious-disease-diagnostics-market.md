<p><p>The infectious disease diagnostics market is comprised of two main types: molecular diagnostic techniques and traditional diagnostic techniques. Molecular diagnostics utilize advanced methods like polymerase chain reaction (PCR) and next-generation sequencing to detect pathogens at a genetic level, offering high sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, traditional diagnostic techniques include culture methods, serological tests, and microscopy, which rely on identifying the pathogen or its antibodies through established biological methods. Both approaches play crucial roles in accurate disease detection and management.</p></p>
