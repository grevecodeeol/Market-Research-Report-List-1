<p><p>Internet of Things (IoT) Vehicle to Vehicle (V2V) communication encompasses three market types: tethered, embedded, and integrated. Tethered systems rely on external devices linked to vehicles, often via smartphones, for connectivity. Embedded solutions come built-in within the vehicle's hardware, enabling direct communication with other vehicles without needing external devices. Integrated markets blend both approaches, offering seamless connectivity through in-vehicle systems that interface with broader IoT networks, enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and real-time data exchange.</p></p>
