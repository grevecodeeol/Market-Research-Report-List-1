<p><p>The IoT communication protocol market features several key types: </p><p>1. **Wi-Fi** offers high-speed internet connectivity, ideal for large data transfers in smart homes and offices.</p><p>2. **Bluetooth** provides low-power short-range communication, commonly used in wearables and gadgets.</p><p>3. **Zigbee** is designed for low-power, low-data rate applications, ideal for smart home devices and industrial automation.</p><p>4. **Bluetooth Smart (BLE)** allows for even greater energy efficiency, enhancing the functionality of portable devices and IoT applications while maintaining a compact range. Each protocol serves distinct use cases based on range, power consumption, and data requirements.</p></p>
