<p><ul><li>Flatbed Scanners</li><li>Paper-fed Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large-format scanners market is primarily divided into two types: flatbed scanners and paper-fed scanners. Flatbed scanners feature a glass surface where documents or images are placed for high-quality scanning, making them ideal for fragile materials and oversized documents. In contrast, paper-fed scanners utilize an automatic document feeder to quickly scan multiple pages in succession, enhancing efficiency for bulk scanning tasks. These scanners cater to diverse sectors including architecture, engineering, and graphic design, driving demand for large-format digitization solutions.</p></p>
