<p><strong>The Meat Moisture Analysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meat moisture analysis equipment market primarily features two types of technologies: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes low-energy x-rays to accurately assess fat and moisture content in meat, providing precise measurements for quality control. In contrast, NIR technology employs the interaction of near-infrared light with meat samples to determine moisture levels quickly and non-destructively. Both methods are essential for ensuring product quality, compliance, and meeting consumer expectations in the meat industry.</p></p>
