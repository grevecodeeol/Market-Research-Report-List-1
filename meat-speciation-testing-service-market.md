<p><p>Meat speciation testing services are critical for verifying the authenticity of meat products. The market includes various testing methods: </p><p>1. **PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)** enables the detection of specific DNA sequences, identifying the species present in meat samples quickly and accurately. </p><p>2. **ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay)** uses antibodies to detect protein markers specific to certain meat types, ensuring reliable species verification. </p><p>3. **Other Molecular Diagnostic Tests, such as LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry),** analyze chemical compounds, providing precise species identification and quantification, enhancing food safety and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
