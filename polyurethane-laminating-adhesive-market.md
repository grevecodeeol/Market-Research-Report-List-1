<p><p>The Polyurethane Laminating Adhesive market is divided into two main types: solvent-based laminating adhesives and solventless laminating adhesives. Solvent-based laminating adhesives contain solvents that help in achieving a strong bond but may release volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising environmental concerns. In contrast, solventless laminating adhesives use a chemical reaction to bond materials without adding solvents, making them more eco-friendly and suitable for various applications, particularly in packaging and laminating industries where regulatory compliance is critical.</p></p>
