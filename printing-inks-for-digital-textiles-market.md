<p><strong>The Printing Inks for Digital Textiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital textile printing market features various ink types catering to different fabrics and applications. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and other cellulosic materials. Acidic inks are suitable for protein fibers like silk and wool, offering vibrant colors. Paint inks are versatile for various surfaces but require pre-treatment. Dispersion inks are designed for synthetic substrates, providing high durability. Sublimation inks transfer prints onto polyester fabrics, creating vivid designs through a heat process, making them popular for apparel and home textiles.</p></p>
