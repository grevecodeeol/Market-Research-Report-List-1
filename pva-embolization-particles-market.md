<p><p>The PVA embolization particles market is categorized into three types: Conventional/Irregular PVA, Compressible PVA, and Spherical PVA. Conventional/Irregular PVA particles are non-uniform in shape, offering versatility in various embolization procedures. Compressible PVA particles can be compacted, enhancing delivery through catheters and improving vascular occlusion. Spherical PVA particles have a uniform shape, providing consistent flow characteristics and predictable embolization outcomes. Each type serves specific clinical applications, catering to diverse patient needs in interventional radiology.</p></p>
