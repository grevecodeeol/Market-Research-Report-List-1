<p><p>The surgical energy instruments market comprises various types designed for precise tissue manipulation and cutting during surgeries. Monopolar instruments utilize a single electrode to deliver electricity, effective for cutting and coagulation. Bipolar instruments feature two electrodes, enabling targeted tissue effects with reduced collateral damage. Ultrasonic instruments leverage high-frequency vibrations to dissect tissue while minimizing heat generation. Other instruments may include advanced energy devices like laser and microwave systems, enhancing surgical efficiency and improving patient outcomes across diverse surgical procedures.</p></p>
