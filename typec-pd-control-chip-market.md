<p><strong>The TypeC PD Control Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UFP</li><li>DFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Type-C Power Delivery (PD) control chip market is segmented into two main types: Upstream Facing Port (UFP) and Downstream Facing Port (DFP). UFP devices typically act as power consumers, commonly found in smartphones and laptops that receive power. In contrast, DFP devices function as power sources, found in chargers and power banks supplying energy to UFP devices. This segmentation influences design, performance, and compatibility in power transfer applications within various electronic devices.</p></p>
