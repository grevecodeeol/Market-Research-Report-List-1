<p><p>Wet chemical scrubbers are essential for removing pollutants from industrial exhausts. Venturi wet scrubbers use a high-speed airflow to enhance particle contact with liquid, effectively capturing contaminants. Packed tower wet scrubbers feature packing materials that improve gas-liquid contact for efficient gas removal. Impingement wet scrubbers operate by forcing gas through a series of liquid jets, promoting extensive interaction. Other types may include spray towers and bubble column scrubbers, each designed for specific applications in air quality control and emissions reduction.</p></p>
