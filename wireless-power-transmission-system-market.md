<p><p>The Wireless Power Transmission System market includes various technologies. Inductive coupling transfers energy through magnetic fields, ideal for shorter distances, like charging electric toothbrushes. Resonant inductive coupling extends this range by using resonant circuits, suitable for electric vehicles. Capacitive coupling employs electric fields to transmit power, effective for small devices at close range. Other methods may include microwave or laser technologies, expanding the application scope for wireless energy transfer in diverse industries, including consumer electronics and automotive sectors.</p></p>
