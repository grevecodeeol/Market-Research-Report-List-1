<p><p>The wireless RFID reader market can be divided into two main types: fixed/wall-mounted and portable readers. Fixed or wall-mounted readers are designed to be permanently installed in specific locations, providing continuous tracking and monitoring of tagged items, often used in warehouses and retail environments. Portable RFID readers, on the other hand, are handheld devices that allow users to scan RFID tags on the go, offering flexibility and mobility for inventory management, asset tracking, and field applications.</p></p>
